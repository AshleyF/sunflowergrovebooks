Little Pita's Adventure

Little Pita's Adventure is a charming children's story about a little girl who goes on an adventure in her imagination. Join her as she stumbles across silly creatures in the garden and meets a new friend, Captain Peter.

Written by Tracey Feniello and edited by Ashley Feniello, this playful adventure is filled with humor and heart as Little Pita explores a whimsical world and discovers some of the silliest fish.
