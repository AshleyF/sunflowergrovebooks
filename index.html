The Chronicles of Sunflower Grove

Sun Bean Chuckles:

A collection of short stories…

“The Chronicles of Sunflower Grove: Sun Bean Chuckles” is a collection of short stories that are set in the coffee shop Sun Bean Coffee, in the town of Sunflower Grove. The little robot barista, Brewster, finds himself in comical situations while trying to blend in with the human world. In “Made with Love” he sets out on an adventure, discovering what love is. So he can infuse his coffee with love. In “Yawning Frenzy” he finds himself in a hilarious situation of the customers having contagious yawns. In “Max & Jasper: A Latte of Laughter,” the coffee shop is left in stitches as the boys find a coffee joke book and read aloud from it. There are a couple of other chapters, but I’ll let you discover those.

Emma’s Artistic Adventure

A Tale of Blueberry Whiskers

“Emma’s Artistic Adventures: A Tale of Blueberry Whiskers” is a charming story about Emma, a creative young girl with a passion for art. Equipped with her sketch pad and paints, she explores a vibrant sunflower field in her town, Sunflower Grove. There, amidst the colorful sunflowers, she finds an unexpected friend — a tiny robot mouse with a blueberry blue body and red checkered ears, whom she names Blueberry Whiskers. This delightful encounter inspires her art. The story beautifully intertwines themes of creativity, nature’s beauty, and the joy of unexpected friendships. Emma’s journey culminates in sharing her artwork and new companion at a local floral shop, celebrating the magic of artistic expression and community spirit.

Postal Predicaments

A collection of short stories…

“The Chronicles of Sunflower Grove: Postal Predicaments” is a collection of short stories that are set in the little post office, in the town of Sunflower Grove. Rusty the robot, a bumbling relic of a bygone era, tends to deliver the mail to wrong house. Hedy, who is hard of hearing, gets the whole town involved in a hilarious scene right out of a circus. Follow along as Max uses some unusual clues to uncover the author of a poem that had been mistakenly delivered to him. This book is filled with short stories that will leave you in stitches.

Charlie and the Ladybug Hunt

“Everyone, It’s a Ladybug!”

Join Charlie in “Charlie and the Ladybug Hunt: Everyone, It’s a Ladybug!” as he searches for the first ladybug of the year. Along the way he bumps into Emma, Sunflower Groves most popular artist. Emma tags along with Charlie in his quest for the first ladybug. With his bug jar in hand, he heads to the rose bushes, where ladybugs like to hide.

In this delightful new addition to the Sunflower Grove series, beloved characters such as Emma, Poppy, Sal and Blueberry Whiskers return to share another adventure.

Wacky Tales and Poems

Tales from The Book Nook

Wacky Tales and Poems: Tales from The Book Nook is a hilarious collection of short stories and poems revolving around Sunflower Grove's charming book store, The Book Nook. Discover how Sonny came to be the most reliable book delivering robot rooster. How Luna’s birthday gift, a pair of roller skates changed her from a windmilling spectacle to the girl who could dance on wheels. Keep up with Sonny if you can, while he’s off delivering books. “Gotta Go! Gotta Go!” Join The Book Nook for poetry hour. If you like poetry that inspires you to dive into your inner wisdom, or maybe you just enjoy poetry that gives you a good laugh, you’ll find it in Rainy Days in The Book Nook. You just might be inspired to write your own poem.
